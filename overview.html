THE WOLRD WIDE WEB

World Wide Web or WWW is the universe of network-accessible information, an embodiment of human knowledge (T, 1989), in other word the World Wide Web is a way of exchanging information between computers on the Internet, putting them together into a huge collection of interactive multimedia resources.

The WWW was created in March 1989 by Berners-Lee. He presented it to the European Organisation for Nuclear Research as an offer for a large hypertext database with typed links. However, the WWW was only implemented at the end of 1990 (T, 2000).

Berners-Lee

Since the day in which the first web site was published, the Web has been growing at an amazing rapid pace. In 2005, this increase has been evaluated to be around seven million new pages a day (Gulli A and Signorini A, 2005). The Web is the most used application in the world with the current estimation size to be around 22 billion pages, it has deeply revolutionized the way people study, communicate, make business, deliver services, relax, etc.
